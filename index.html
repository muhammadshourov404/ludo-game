How to Play

🎯 Objective

Move all 4 of your tokens from home to the center of the board before your opponents.

🎲 Rolling

Roll a 6 to bring a token out of home. Roll again on any 6!

⚔ Cutting

Land on an opponent's token to send it back to home. Safe squares are protected.

⚡ Rush Mode

15 seconds per turn. Run out of time = skip!

✨ Magical Mode

Collect magic cards. Use them for special powers like teleport, shield, and reverse!

🃏 Magic Cards

🚀Boost — Move +3 extra steps
🛡Shield — Block one attack
🌀Swap — Swap position with opponent
🔄Reverse — Reverse an opponent's last move
⚡Double — Move twice this turn
❄Freeze — Freeze opponent for 1 turn
